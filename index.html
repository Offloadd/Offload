Welcome to Offload

Offload helps you track your emotional tolerance and nervous system state in real-time.

Use the sliders to indicate your current internal state across different life areas. The visualization shows your window of tolerance - when you're regulated, stressed, or experiencing opportunities for growth.

How to use:

  • Adjust sliders from left (+5, positive) to right (-5, negative)
  • Add specific experiences in Section 3
  • Watch the visualization update in real-time
  • Save entries to track patterns over time

Start wherever feels right - there's no wrong way to begin.
